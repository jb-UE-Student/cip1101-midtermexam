Why We Rock!

Blessed with beautiful natural sceneries, Sunnies Sky Beach Resort is located in Sunnies Sky Island, Palawan, Phillipines. Sunnies Sky Beach Resort is attracting attention for the best spot for family vacations as well as business meetings.

This family-friendly resort takes enjoyment to the fullest with premium guestrooms with ocean view, along with private beach, extensive pools, luxurious massage facility, beach-side restaurant, bar, conference room, gym, and library equipped with not only books and computers but also recreation facilities.
